Pancakes

Description

You mix flour, milk,a little baking powder and 2 eggs toggle the together.Then you put it in the pan for 1 minute,on medium heat, and flip half way through.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 500g flour
  • 2 tea spoons baking powder
  • 500ml milk
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3 teaspoon sugar

Steps

  1. Add all wet ingredients together
  2. Add all dry ingredients together
  3. through wet solution in with dry ingredients
  4. Mix until no more dry ingrediants can be seen(its okay to make lumps)
  5. pour solution ,Amount to your liking, on to oiled pan.
  6. Cook for 30 seconds on each side of pancake for 30 seconds on medium heat
